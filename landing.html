Problem Statement

Many students struggle to keep up with lessons, and long, text-heavy materials often cause disengagement. A 2024 Philippine Statistics Authority survey found a 93.1% basic literacy rate but only 70.8% functional literacy — showing many Filipinos still struggle to apply what they learn. This gap isn't unique to the Philippines; several countries, especially in sub-Saharan Africa, face similar literacy challenges.

Proposed Solution

EduQueSCT pairs summarized content, visual aids, and interactive quizzes to boost comprehension and keep students engaged. The game combines learning with gamification across three stage types — a platformer, a maze, and a side-scroller. Correct answers earn points; wrong answers come with explanations to reinforce the lesson.

AI Incorporation

Gemini AI, Claude, Google Flow, ChatGPT, and Figma helped shape a more dynamic and accessible learning experience. AI generated quiz questions across difficulty levels and topics, assisted with visuals, and powered dynamic maps and challenges — introducing new paths and obstacles that keep gameplay fresh and unpredictable.

Result & Impact on Education and the Environment

EduQueSCT is a free, browser-based review game covering Math, English/Reading, and Science for grades 7–10, playable on computers and mobile devices. Beyond academics, it replaces paper-based study sheets — cutting down on paper waste — so studying becomes more convenient and more sustainable.
